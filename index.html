Latitude vs. X

The purpose of this project was to evaluate weather trends at different latitudes. The specific weather variables examined were temperature, cloudiness, precipitation, and humidity

Data was collected from OpenWeatherAPI, and was subsequently cleaned and formatted with Pandas. The accompanying visualizations were prepared with Matplotlib.

So take a look around. We have plenty of visualizations on weather patterns across latitude, as well as a data section to view the dataset.
